Classes & Programs

From your very first arrow to competitive shooting — find the course that fits your goals, schedule, and budget.

Beginner Classes

New to archery? Our beginner programs are designed to give you a safe, supportive, and fun introduction to the sport. Equipment is provided — just show up ready to learn.

A101 — Introduction to Archery

The perfect starting point for anyone new to archery. This course covers foundational archery safety, proper stance and posture, grip, draw, and release technique. Participants will shoot at 10-yard targets to build confidence and consistency.

None — all welcome

All equipment provided

Intermediate Classes

Ready to level up? Intermediate classes refine your technique, improve consistency, and help you select personal equipment suited to your style and goals.

A102 — Skill Development

Building directly on A101, this course focuses on refining technique, improving shot consistency, and introducing archers to 20-yard shooting. Students will also receive guidance on personal equipment selection — covering bow styles, arrows, and accessories suited to their individual goals.

Schedule
Tuesdays, 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM
Cost
$90 per 4-week session
Prerequisites
Completion of A101 or equivalent
Lane Distance
10 & 20 yards
Enroll in A102

Advanced & Competitive

For serious athletes ready to compete. Our advanced program aligns with WSAA and NFAA regulations and prepares you for tournament-level performance.

A103 — Competitive Training

This advanced course transitions skilled archers into tournament-style shooting. Training covers precision technique, mental focus, competitive scoring formats, and official regulations as recognized by the Washington State Archery Association (WSAA) and National Field Archery Association (NFAA). Lane distances of 10 and 20 yards are used throughout.

Schedule
Thursdays, 6:00 PM – 8:30 PM
Cost
$110 per 4-week session
Prerequisites
Completion of A102 or pre-evaluation
Lane Distance
10 & 20 yards (standard competition)
Enroll in A103
