Archery Classes

From first-time archers to tournament-focused athletes, our classes are organized by skill level so you can join the right training path.

Class Offerings

A101 Basic Archery

Ideal for beginners learning range safety, stance, aiming, and release fundamentals in a supportive environment.

  • Schedule: Monday and Wednesday, 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM
  • Cost: $120 per month
  • Prerequisites: None; ages 8+ (age 7 accepted with evaluation)
  • Equipment: Basic gear provided for new students

A102 Intermediate Archery

Designed for archers who are ready to improve accuracy, consistency, and shot execution after completing beginner fundamentals.

  • Schedule: Tuesday and Thursday, 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM
  • Cost: $120 per month
  • Prerequisites: Completion of A101 or instructor approval
  • Equipment: Students may use personal or center-provided equipment

A103 Advanced Archery

Focuses on advanced form refinement, competition preparation, and technical performance coaching for experienced archers.

  • Schedule: Tuesday and Thursday, 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM
  • Cost: $120 per month
  • Prerequisites: Completion of A102 or instructor approval
  • Equipment: Guidance available for selecting competition-ready gear
