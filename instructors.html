Our Coaching Staff

Every instructor at Straight to the Point Archery holds USA Archery certification and is committed to creating a safe, welcoming, and high-quality learning environment for students of all ages and abilities.

Derek Jenkins

Certification
Level 3 Coach
Coaching Bow Styles
Recurve
"My biggest goal is to see archery grow and help its participants to grow with it."

Derek has been competing and coaching in the recurve discipline for over 15 years. He discovered archery as a teenager and has never looked back. Outside the range, Derek enjoys hiking the Cascades and volunteering with youth outdoor programs. His patient, methodical teaching style makes him especially popular with beginners and young archers.

Sarah Okafor

Certification
Level 3 Coach — USA Archery
Coaching Bow Styles
Compound & Recurve
"Archery teaches you patience, precision, and presence — skills that go far beyond the range."

Sarah is a two-time state medalist in the compound division and brings competitive expertise to every session she coaches. With a background in sports psychology, she helps students develop not just technique but the mental resilience needed to perform under pressure. Sarah also leads our A103 competitive training program.

Marcus Tanaka

Certification
Level 2 Coach — USA Archery
Coaching Bow Styles
Traditional Longbow & Barebow
"There's something deeply satisfying about the traditional form — no sights, no stabilizers, just instinct and practice."

Marcus specializes in traditional archery styles, including longbow and barebow shooting. He is the go-to coach for students interested in bow hunting, historical archery, or anyone who wants to develop instinctive shooting. In his free time, Marcus practices 3D archery in the woods and teaches wilderness survival skills.

Linda Cartwright

Certification
Level 2 Coach — USA Archery
Coaching Bow Styles
Recurve & Youth Programs
"Every child who walks in here leaves with more confidence than they came with. That's what keeps me going."

With a background in elementary education, Linda brings remarkable skill in working with children and first-time archers. She leads our youth programs, scouts sessions, and introductory A101 classes. Linda is passionate about inclusive sports education and ensuring every young person feels capable and welcome at the range.
