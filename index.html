Our Mission

Straight to the Point Archery was founded to make the sport of archery accessible, safe, and rewarding for every member of our community. As a non-profit archery center based in Seattle, WA, we believe that anyone — regardless of age, background, or experience — deserves the chance to discover the discipline and joy of archery.

We are affiliated with the Washington State Archery Association (WSAA) and the National Field Archery Association (NFAA), ensuring our training aligns with official standards.
