Why Start Here

  • Beginner classes designed for first-time archers.
  • Youth options for ages 8+ (age 7 with evaluation).
  • USA Archery-certified coaches and a safety-first environment.

Quick Answers for New Students and Families

  • Safety first: Every class begins with range safety rules and supervised instruction.
  • What to bring: New students can start with center-provided beginner equipment.
  • Age guidance: Youth classes are available for ages 8+ (age 7 accepted with evaluation).
  • Facility access: Train on indoor 10-yard and 20-yard lanes with space for progressing archers.
  • Credentials: Coaching follows standards from recognized archery organizations.
