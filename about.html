About Straight to the Point Archery

We are a Tacoma-based non-profit archery education center committed to creating a safe, welcoming, and growth-focused environment for every archer.

Our Mission

Our mission is to teach archery with integrity, safety, and purpose while helping students of all ages build confidence, focus, and lifelong skills.

We support beginners, youth, and experienced athletes through structured training, certified coaching, and a strong commitment to positive learning experiences.

Our History

Founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen, Straight to the Point Archery began as a community-driven non-profit focused on expanding access to quality archery instruction.

Over the years, the center has grown into a trusted local training destination for recreational students, youth programs, and competitive archers.

Our Facilities

  • 28 indoor lanes, including both 10-yard and 20-yard distances
  • 2 private training rooms for focused one-on-one coaching
  • Additional range space with expanded lane access and an outdoor practice field
  • Instructional environments for youth classes, private lessons, and group sessions

Community and Partnerships

We actively serve schools, scouts, and local organizations through inclusive programs that introduce archery in a supportive and educational setting.

Our center maintains affiliations with recognized archery and bowhunting organizations to align instruction with established standards.

Policies and Commitment

Straight to the Point Archery upholds a nondiscriminatory policy and welcomes participants of all backgrounds, identities, and abilities.

We are committed to maintaining a respectful, safe, and community-centered environment where every student can learn and thrive.
