About Straight to the Point Archery

A community-rooted, non-profit archery center in Seattle, WA — dedicated to growing the sport and everyone who loves it.

Our Mission

"To make archery accessible, safe, and rewarding for every member of our community — regardless of age, background, or experience."

Straight to the Point Archery was founded on a simple belief: that archery is a sport for everyone. As a non-profit organization, we reinvest in our programs, our facility, and our community rather than pursuing profit. Every class fee, every membership, and every donation goes back into creating the best possible environment for archers at all levels.

We believe in the power of archery to build focus, self-discipline, confidence, and community. From a child shooting their first arrow to a competitive athlete preparing for a national tournament, we are committed to supporting every archer's journey.

Our History

Straight to the Point Archery was founded by a lifelong archer and community organizer who saw a gap in Seattle's recreational sports landscape. What began as a small weekend club in a local community center grew into a fully equipped indoor archery range offering structured programming for all ages.

Over the years, we have expanded our lane capacity, added youth programs, partnered with community organizations, and earned affiliations with the Washington State Archery Association (WSAA) and the National Field Archery Association (NFAA) — ensuring our training standards meet official competitive guidelines.

Today, we serve hundreds of archers each year, from curious beginners to WSAA competitors, and we remain as committed to our founding mission as ever.

Our Facility

Our indoor facility is designed with both safety and performance in mind. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a veteran competitor, you'll find everything you need to train comfortably and effectively.

 Range

10 & 20-Yard Lanes

Multiple indoor lanes at both 10-yard and 20-yard distances, accommodating beginner practice and standard competition distances used by WSAA and NFAA.

 Equipment

Loaner Equipment Available

We provide bows, arrows, and protective equipment for students in all beginner and intermediate classes. Personal equipment is welcome and encouraged at the advanced level.

 Safety

Safety-First Design

Our facility is laid out to ensure maximum safety for all participants. All ranges are supervised, and every session begins with a safety briefing led by a certified instructor.

 Accessibility

Accessible to All

Our facility is ADA-accessible and designed to welcome archers of all physical abilities. Please contact us in advance if you have specific accommodation needs.

Affiliations & Standards

Our programs are aligned with the leading organizations in the sport, so you can be confident that your training meets official competitive standards.

USA Archery

USA Archery Certification

All of our coaches are certified through USA Archery, the national governing body for the sport. This ensures consistent, high-quality, safety-conscious instruction.

WSAA

Washington State Archery Association

As a WSAA-affiliated center, our advanced programs prepare archers for state-level competition and follow all official rules and formats.

NFAA

National Field Archery Association

Our affiliation with the NFAA means our training aligns with national field archery standards, supporting students who aspire to compete at a national level.

Our Policies

Non-Discrimination Policy

Straight to the Point Archery is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all participants. We do not discriminate on the basis of:

  • Race, color, or national origin
  • Gender identity or sexual orientation
  • Age (we welcome participants age 7 and up)
  • Religion or cultural background
  • Physical disability or ability level

Every archer who walks through our doors deserves to feel safe, respected, and supported. If you ever experience or witness behavior that violates this standard, please contact us immediately.

Safety Policy

Safety is our highest priority. All participants are required to:

  • Complete a safety orientation before their first session
  • Follow all range commands given by instructors
  • Never draw a bow when others are downrange
  • Wear appropriate protective equipment as directed
  • Report any unsafe conditions or incidents to staff immediately

Violation of safety rules may result in removal from the facility. We take this seriously to protect all members of our community.

Registration & Cancellation

Class registration is confirmed upon payment. Cancellations made 7 or more days before a session begins are eligible for a full refund. Please contact us for extenuating circumstances or rescheduling requests.

Ready to Join Our Community?

Whether you're brand new to archery or looking for a community to grow with, we'd love to meet you. Come see the facility, meet the coaches, and find the right program for you.
